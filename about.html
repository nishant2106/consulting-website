We are

Acco Global Consultancy is a vision of 2 Chartered Accountants to grow and excel in the Finance & Accounting (F&A) Outsourcing industry. We Acco Global as an Outsourcing Service Provider have been consistent in delivering for the past 3 years our best expertized tailor-made solutions to our clients for their Finance and Accounts needs. We are passionate about the services we render and help businesses to outperform through our expertise in (F&A) which help them in crucial decision making which can impact their finances. It is our aim to let our clients analyse their business performance on a real time basis with the help of cloud-based accounting. We also assist our clients in their financial planning for a better financial health of the enterprise.

Mission & Vision

Mission of Acco Global

To increase our global footprint in more and more nations across the world by being real-time adaptive to the newest technology trends in the Financial & Accounting sector and new ways of accounting.
To multiply our distribution centers so that we can improve turnaround times and expand our client base. Our distribution centers are currently placed throughout India's major cities. To keep our infrastructure solid and all software platforms in-house.
Our mission is to seek out and seize chances to provide exceptional services to our clients that go beyond cost savings. Acco Global aspires to play a key role in our clients' value chain and profit- generating activities.

Vision of Acco Global

To be recognized as the world's leading Consulting Company with Centre of Excellence, providing premium accounting, financial & reporting services and taxation services to businesses all over the world.

Core Value

Excellence. Work quality does not stop at competence; it goes above and beyond what clients anticipate.
Commitment. Our team's commitment is aligned with the company's vision to the betterment and success of our clients, allocating the necessary resources and training to help you achieve your objectives.

Culture a winning environment where we achieve business results while balancing the personal pursuit of family time, spiritual growth and physical well-being.
